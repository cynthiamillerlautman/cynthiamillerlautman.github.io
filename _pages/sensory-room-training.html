Sensory Room Training

This introductory Sensory Room Training will allow participants who are interested in learning more about basic sensory rooms and how to set them up in their schools, daycares or homes.

The following will be covered in the course:

  • Introductory sensory experience.
  • Why is sensory development so important?
  • Materials needed for a basic sensory room.
  • Equipment safety:
    • Weighted blankets
    • Suspended and rotary equipment
  • Suggestions for movement break activies
  • Signs of overload
